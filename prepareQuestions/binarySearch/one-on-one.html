Shubham and Arun have a one on one project . They want to provide shade along some path of length len, consisting of n bulbs. Consider the path as x-axis starting from origin, and its end len. Then the j-th tree is at the point aj. The tree all have a shade power of p where p denotes the distance to where the tree can provide shade.

Arun wonders: what is the minimum shade power p should the trees have to shade the path from 0 to len?
